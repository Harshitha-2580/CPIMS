About NEC Placement Cell

The NEC Placement Cell is the Training, Placement, and Career Development center of Narayana Engineering College. The cell plays a crucial role in guiding students towards successful careers by connecting them with leading companies and industry opportunities. It acts as a bridge between students and recruiters by organizing campus recruitment drives, career counseling sessions, and industry interaction programs.
