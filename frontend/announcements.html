Record Placement Season: 450+ Offers Achieved!

Published: October 1, 2025

We are thrilled to announce a phenomenal placement season for the 2024-25 batch! As of today, our students have secured **450+ job offers**. This record-breaking tally includes 15 offers above the 20 LPA mark.

**Key Highlights:**

  • **Total Offers:** 450+
  • **Companies Visited:** 125+
  • **Highest Package:** ₹29 LPA (offered by Belc Co Ltd)
  • **Major Recruiters:** TCS, Infosys, Cognizant, Wipro, and numerous Global Capability Centers (GCCs).

Tech Giant 'DataSynth' Recruits 25 CSE/IT Graduates!

Published: September 28, 2025

Leading Data Analytics firm, **DataSynth**, conducted a successful campus drive last week, resulting in the selection of 25 final-year students from the CSE and IT branches. This marks a significant collaboration, with offers averaging **₹8.5 LPA**. Congratulations to all selected candidates!

Aspira Ranks in Top 50 for Career Readiness by FutureTech Index

Published: September 15, 2025

Narayana Engineering College, Nellore, through the continuous efforts of the Aspira cell, has been ranked among the **Top 50 Engineering Colleges for Career Readiness** in India by the prestigious FutureTech Index 2025. This achievement highlights our commitment to bridging the skill gap and fostering industry-ready talent. [Image of Gold Award Trophy]

Core Sector Triumph: 42 Students Selected by Mechanical & Civil Giants

Published: August 20, 2025

The core engineering branches (Mechanical and Civil) showed exceptional performance in recent drives. Companies like L&T Construction and Siemens India hired a total of 42 students for entry-level positions. This diverse recruitment trend ensures opportunities across all disciplines.
