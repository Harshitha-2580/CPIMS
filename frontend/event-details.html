CodeSpark Hackathon: Build the Future 🚀

The **CodeSpark Hackathon** is a 24-hour coding challenge designed to push your problem-solving skills and creativity. Teams will tackle real-world industry problems in areas like sustainable technology, fintech, and automation. This is a prime opportunity to demonstrate technical prowess to leading recruiters.

Participants will work in teams of up to 4, with access to mentorship from senior faculty and industry experts. The winning team receives a cash prize and guaranteed fast-track consideration from our top recruiting partners.

Event Tracks & Core Objectives

This year’s event features three primary tracks:

  • **Sustainable Tech:** Develop solutions for energy management or waste reduction.
  • **Fintech Innovations:** Build prototypes for secure and efficient digital payments.
  • **AI for Automation:** Create smart bots or automated processes for enterprises.

Eligibility and Requirements

Participation is restricted to students who meet the following criteria:

  • **Eligibility Branches:** B.Tech/M.Tech (Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering).
  • **College Years:** 3rd and 4th Year Students only.
  • Basic knowledge of version control (Git) and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) is mandatory.
