Top Recruiter Spotlight & Historical Data

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

IT Services Mass Recruiter

TCS is one of India's largest IT services companies and a cornerstone recruiter at NECN. Their history with the college spans over a decade, hiring students primarily for software development, infrastructure management, and business process services. TCS offers roles to students across CSE, IT, ECE, and even core engineering branches for technical profiles.

Total Hires (Last 3 Yrs): 180+ students. **Recruits for:** CSE, IT, ECE, MECH, EEE, CIVIL.

Infosys

Global Consulting Digital Innovation

A global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. Infosys targets students with strong coding skills and analytical abilities. They recruit for roles including System Engineer and Digital Specialist, frequently visiting our campus multiple times per placement cycle.

Total Hires (Last 3 Yrs): 150+ students. **Recruits for:** CSE, IT, ECE, MECH, EEE.

DataSynth Analytics

Data Science High Package Offer

DataSynth is a specialist firm in AI and Big Data solutions. They recruit a select group of highly skilled students from CSE and IT, known for offering some of the highest package offers (up to ₹29 LPA). Their focus is on core data science, machine learning, and cloud architecture roles.

Recent Hires (2024-25): 25 students. **Recruits for:** CSE, IT.

Larsen & Toubro (L&T) Construction

Core Infrastructure Civil/Mechanical

L&T is an Indian multinational infrastructure and technology conglomerate. They are the key recruiter for our core engineering branches. They look for students in Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering for positions like Site Engineer, Project Management, and Design roles.

Recent Hires (2024-25): 20 students. **Recruits for:** CIVIL, MECH, EEE.
